About AgeCalc

AgeCalc was built with one mission: give anyone a free, instant, and accurate way to calculate their exact age down to the day — no sign-up required, no fees, no clutter.

Most age calculators online are buried inside multi-tool websites, slow to load, and stuffed with distracting ads right next to the input fields. We believe calculating your age should be as simple as entering a date and reading the result.

How It Works

AgeCalc runs entirely in your browser. When you enter a birth date, JavaScript calculates the difference between that date and today (or any target date you choose) using precise Gregorian calendar math — including leap year handling. Your data never leaves your device.

Why We Built It

We needed a simple age calculator ourselves and were frustrated with the existing options. So we made one that we would actually want to use: fast, clean, mobile-friendly, and respectful of your time.

Privacy

AgeCalc does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything is computed locally in your browser. We don't use cookies for tracking. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
