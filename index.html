How to Use the Age Calculator

  1. Enter your date of birth in the first field using the date picker or by typing it in.

Why Calculate Your Exact Age?

Knowing your precise age down to the day matters more than you might think. Schools require exact ages for enrollment. Legal documents often need your age as of a specific date. Insurance and retirement calculations depend on accurate birth-to-date math. And for parents, tracking a child's age in months is essential for pediatric growth charts and school cutoff dates.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the calculator handle leap years?
The calculator accounts for leap years automatically. It uses the Gregorian calendar rules, counting February 29 only in leap years.
Can I calculate age for a future date?
Yes. Set a target date in the future and the calculator will show how old someone will be on that date.
What if I was born on February 29?
The calculator handles leap day birthdays correctly. Your "legal" birthday falls on March 1 in non-leap years in most jurisdictions.
Is my data saved?
No. All calculations happen in your browser. We don't store, transmit, or log your birth date or any personal information.
